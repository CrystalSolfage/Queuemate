Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get a QueueMate?

Most bookings are confirmed within 15-30 minutes, depending on location and time of day. During peak hours, we recommend booking at least 1 hour in advance.

What if my QueueMate is late?

If your QueueMate is more than 15 minutes late without communication, we'll either replace them immediately or refund your Qbucks for the delayed time.

How do payments work for QueueMates?

QueueMates earn R2.20 per minute of waiting time. Payments are processed weekly to your bank account every Friday for work completed the previous week.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees. You only pay for the actual time spent waiting (rounded up to the nearest minute). There's a 2-minute minimum charge per booking.
