How QueueMate Works

Whether you're a customer looking to save time or want to earn as a QueueMate, we've got you covered.

For Customers

Get started in minutes

Skip the wait with our simple 3-step process

1. Book via WhatsApp

Message us at +27 79 222 4548 with your location and estimated wait time.

2. QueueMate assigned

We'll confirm your booking and assign a QueueMate within minutes.

3. Get notified

Receive updates when your QueueMate arrives and when it's your turn.

Using Qbucks

QueueMate uses Qbucks as currency (1 Qbuck = 1 minute). Purchase Qbucks bundles or subscribe for unlimited access. Learn more about pricing

For QueueMates

Earn money on your schedule

Flexible work with transparent earnings

1. Join our team

Register via WhatsApp and join our QueueMate workers group.

2. Accept jobs

Receive job alerts and respond with ACCEPT or REJECT.

3. Get paid

Earn R2.20 per minute. Weekly payouts to your bank account.

QueueMate Rules

  • Only respond to job alerts with ACCEPT or REJECT
  • Jobs are assigned first come, first serve
  • Update status messages: ARRIVED → WAITING → COMPLETE
  • Weekly payouts tracked and shared by admin
