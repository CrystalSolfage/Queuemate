How do I purchase Qbucks?
Simply message us on WhatsApp (+27 62 709 6838) and we'll guide you through the purchase process. You can pay via EFT or mobile payment.
Do Qbucks expire?
No, your Qbucks balance never expires. Use them whenever you need our services.
What happens if my QueueMate waits longer than estimated?
We'll notify you if the wait is longer than expected. You'll only be charged for the actual time waited.
Can I get a refund for unused Qbucks?
Yes, unused Qbucks can be refunded minus a 10% processing fee. Contact our support team for assistance.
